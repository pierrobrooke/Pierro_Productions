Mark concepts

From the meeting point on the wing

Where the green dappled scales meet the black-ringed pink, abstracted into four marks. Each one is live, animated SVG — open "view code" on any card to copy it straight into Figma or a codebase.

Recommended primary mark

Eye Mark

The eyespot, distilled to three rings. Works at favicon size. Ties straight to "I see your frequency before you do."

View SVG code 
<svg viewBox="0 0 100 100" class="mark mark-eye">
  <circle cx="50" cy="50" r="44" fill="none" stroke="#F6E5D3" stroke-width="1" opacity="0.25"/>
  <g class="eye-rotate">
    <circle cx="50" cy="50" r="34" fill="none" stroke="#8FAC7E" stroke-width="7" stroke-dasharray="1 7" stroke-linecap="round"/>
  </g>
  <circle cx="50" cy="50" r="20" fill="#F8CCC9"/>
  <circle cx="50" cy="50" r="9" fill="#E73788" class="eye-core"/>
</svg>

Vein Knot

Lines converge, then bloom. Good for loading states, section dividers, a "thinking" indicator.

View SVG code 
<svg viewBox="0 0 100 100" class="mark mark-vein">
  <path class="vein-line" d="M10,15 Q40,28 50,50" pathLength="1"/>
  <path class="vein-line" d="M90,18 Q62,32 50,50" pathLength="1"/>
  <path class="vein-line" d="M50,95 Q50,72 50,50" pathLength="1"/>
  <circle cx="44" cy="45" r="1.6" fill="#8FAC7E" opacity=".8"/>
  <circle cx="57" cy="43" r="1.3" fill="#8FAC7E" opacity=".6"/>
  <circle cx="50" cy="50" r="0" fill="#E73788" class="vein-bloom"/>
</svg>

Convergence Dot

The seam between the two textures, always slowly turning. Good as an ambient corner mark or page-load spinner.

View SVG code 
<svg viewBox="0 0 100 100" class="mark mark-conv">
  <defs>
    <clipPath id="convLeft"><rect x="0" y="0" width="50" height="100"/></clipPath>
  </defs>
  <g class="conv-spin">
    <circle cx="50" cy="50" r="36" fill="#E73788"/>
    <g clip-path="url(#convLeft)">
      <circle cx="50" cy="50" r="36" fill="#18181A"/>
      <circle cx="30" cy="36" r="2" fill="#8FAC7E"/>
      <circle cx="22" cy="48" r="1.6" fill="#8FAC7E"/>
      <circle cx="34" cy="58" r="1.8" fill="#8FAC7E"/>
      <circle cx="26" cy="66" r="1.4" fill="#8FAC7E"/>
      <circle cx="36" cy="42" r="1.3" fill="#8FAC7E"/>
    </g>
    <line x1="50" y1="14" x2="50" y2="86" stroke="#F6E5D3" stroke-width="1" opacity=".35"/>
  </g>
</svg>

Scatter to Solid

Scattered becomes whole, breathing in reverse of the center. Good for a hover state on cards or CTAs, not a static mark.
